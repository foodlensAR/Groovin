Our Mission

We at FoodLens offer a menu visualization service by means of implementing Augmented Reality principles. FoodLens uses cutting edge Photogrammetry technology as a technique to obtain reliable data of real-world objects in the environment by creating 3D models from photos that we capture. We use this technology to create 3D models of food and drink items on behalf of some of Ireland's most popular bars and restaurants. You may have guessed our team here at FoodLens are passionate about food, dining and social events! We want to not only create a means of accurately portraying food items on offer, but it is also essential for us to offer a means to bridge the barrier of communication between customer & business. FoodLens is a service which was built on passionate ideas, integrity & a strong sense of togetherness. We work closely with businesses who we believe share these same values & through our community page, they can share these values with you - the user! And of course you can reap the benefits of being up-to-date with the latest events, promotions and announcements from our associated restaurants and bars! FoodLens believes universal accessibility is of the utmost importance in any service. That is why each of the menu items which we display on the app is required to be accompanied by accurate audio descriptions & allergens are also clearly started. We are committed to maintaining this ethos throughout our development. We hope that you enjoy the future of dining in Dublin as much as we enjoyed creating it.
