Highlights from Luna

Luna is contemporary Italian & Cocktail Bar where you can experience top quality fine dining. It's located on Drury Street in Dublin city centre. FoodLens are thrilled to get the opportunity to work with Luna on this project. Seven items from their menu were photographed in total, these were:

  • Beef Tartare Cannelloni
  • Panna Cotta
  • Lightly Charred Seabream
  • Crab Roll & Dingle Bay Prawn
  • Strawberry Dessert
  • Charred Peach Cocktail
