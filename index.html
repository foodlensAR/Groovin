    About The FoodLens Team

    FoodLens was founded by an independent group of Computer Science Masters students at Trinity College with a shared passion for technological innovation and food, who saw a gap in the market for an app which we believe to be ground-breaking in food visualisation, marketing and event planning. The inspiration behind the app was wanting to provide a platform for restaurants to visually showcase their menu items with the latest technology, while providing lots of value to users through storytelling, allergen information, 360 degree views of the dishes, upcoming events and promotions. Augmented reality can be a great tool to promote restaurants, enhancing their relationship with their customers. The inclusion of an immersive technology such as AR, into an already positive experience can really solidify the relationship between business and customer, which helps in increasing customer retention, as well as conversion rates by attracting new customers.

    Our Services

    FoodLens looks at bridging the gap between what consumers want and what restaurants want to showcase, through the following services:

    Menu Visualisation

    Consumers like to know that the product they're going to receive is of a satisfactory standard, visualising menu items helps with this. With FoodLens, we take the uncertainty out of what you order through Augmented Reality - using high resolution, virtual 3D models, allowing users to eat with their eyes.

    Information Sharing

    Providing information through Augmented Reality includes allergen information & wine pairings for dishes on the menu. Some participating restaurants will provide a production narrative. Showing brand transparency & immersing customers in the brand makes the experience more interactive.

    Storytelling

    FoodLens offers restaurants the opportunity to share the inspiration behind each dish & drink. This helps to strengthen the bond between consumers & businesses, creating a mutually beneficial partnership where everyone wins. Immersing consumers in the ‘story’ is a huge part of retaining & attracting new customers.

    Elevate the Dining Experience

    FoodLens aim to improve the dining experience for consumers through AR. Establishments will leave a great impression on their customers, & will attract new customers. Restaurants can do this by providing recommendations for food/drink pairings. This elevates the gastronomic experience by optimising flavours as much as possible, while also allowing restaurants emphasise their broad offerings.

    Community

    This feature allows restaurants to really engage & interact with their customers by letting them be the first to know about special discounts & offers that are available. It’s also where upcoming events in participating restaurants will be advertised, which is beneficial to both restaurants & consumers alike.

    Audio Companion

    FoodLens is accessible for people with visual impairments who might not be able to view the quality of our 3D models, that's why we have added an audio companion to talk users through what each dish contains, it's allergens, wine pairing & any other key points.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Checkout the answers below to some of our most frequently asked questions. If the answer you’re looking for isn’t below, please don’t hesitate to get in touch with us.

    • What is Augmented Reality?

      Augmented reality (AR) is an interactive experience of a real-world environment where the objects that reside in the real world are enhanced by computer generated perceptual information, sometimes across multiple sensory modalities, including visual, auditory, haptic, somatosensory and olfactory.

    • What is FoodLens?

      FoodLens is an app founded by an independent group of Computer Science Masters students which we believe to be ground breaking in food visualisation, marketing and event planning. It allows for menu visualisation through augmented reality.

    • Is allergen information available for the menu items?

      Allergen information will be available for all menu items for each of our participating restaurants, in correspondence with the requirements of the FSAI. Responsibility for providing this information lies with the restaurants.

    • Can I share my FoodLens experience with friends and family on social media?

      Of course - social sharing is very important to us at FoodLens. Be sure to tag us on @ and the restaurant you’re in also, as we would love to see what you’re up to. There is a sharing button within the app which allows you to take a snapshot of your current screen, it saves to your camera roll, and you can share to the social media channel of your choice!
